Who We Are

ABOUT IRONPEAK

Founded in 2015, IronPeak has grown from a small local gym into the city's most respected fitness community. We believe in more than just lifting weights — we build people.

Our Story

10 YEARS OF
BUILDING CHAMPIONS

IronPeak started with a simple vision: create a gym that combines world-class facilities with a community that lifts each other up. Our founder, a former competitive athlete, wanted a space where everyone — beginners and pros alike — would feel at home.

Over the past decade, we've helped over 5,000 members lose weight, build muscle, compete in events, and most importantly, develop healthy habits that last a lifetime.

Today, our 10,000 sq ft facility houses state-of-the-art equipment, a dedicated group fitness studio, a recovery zone, and a nutrition bar — everything under one roof.
